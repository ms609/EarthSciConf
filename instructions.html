Presenter instructions

Oral presentations

Level 4 students will be given instructions on length of presentation and content by the Module Co-ordinator (Ed Llewellin).

Poster presentations

  • Poster boards are landscape but you can put portrait posters up on these
  • Max. poster dimensions: 0.84m height x 1.14m width
  • All posters should be on display throughout the meeting
  • Please put your poster up by 10am on 11 June; your poster number will be in the meeting programme and abstract volume
  • Presenters are requested to stand by their poster in the designated poster sessions:
    • POSTER SESSION 1 (LUNCH): Staff and Postdoctoral Fellows/RAs
    • POSTER SESSION 2 (AFTERNOON): PhD students and Undergraduates
  • Undergraduates - if you need help with poster printing costs, please state this in the email when you submit your abstract.
Poster evaluation:

At least one colleague will evaluate your poster during the poster sessions. Each delegate will be assigned at least 1 poster to review. Poster number and the evaluation form for each poster will be given to participants at the registration desk upon their arrival. The form will include a number of criteria to be evaluated, including the scientific quality of the presentation, the design and the author's ability to answer questions, with marks from 0 to 10. The reviewers will be anonymous and will fill in their evaluation form AFTER viewing your poster, and return it to the registration desk. Each poster author will be provided with their review/s by the organising committee, no later than 2 weeks after the conference. It is important that you stand by your poster at the allotted time so that the reviewer/s can talk to you!

Three minute thesis competition:

Before the conference you should submit a single powerpoint slide to earthscience.conference@durham.ac.uk by the deadline. The competition will be in TR3 (ES231) between 15:30 and 16:30. You should make sure you are in the room for the entire hour as the presentations will lead directly on from each other - be ready to talk as soon as the previous presenter has finished! The running order for the competition will be available at registration on the day of the conference.

Exhibitors

Please set up your stall BEFORE the first coffee break (10:30am). Please liaise with a committee member (at the registration desk at the entrance to TR1) on arrival.
