Welcome to the Eighth Durham University Earth Sciences Conference

Celebrating excellence in our research

The Durham University Earth Sciences Conference will be held on Tuesday 9th June 2020 in teaching rooms 1–3, on level two of the Arthur Holmes Building. All members of the Department and the Science Faculty are encouraged to participate.

The oral presentations will be given by the Level 4 students, as summative presentations of research projects. Everyone is encouraged to present a poster on any aspect of their research, whether it be an undergraduate assignment/dissertation or a presentation given at an academic conference.

Also, this year we will be running a three minute thesis competition, open to all. Can you summarise your PhD, undergraduate dissertation, or current research project in a strict time limit of three minutes and one PowerPoint slide?

To attend the day, you must register, but conference attendance is free.

Meeting highlights

  • Oral presentations by Level 4 dissertation students
  • Two dedicated poster sessions
  • Industry exhibition/recruitment stands
  • Rapid Research competition
